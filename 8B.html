Schedule and Assignment

Important information about when work is due
  • There is a reading assignment and associated reading quiz due for (almost) every class session.  Reading assignments are listed in the schedule table below, and the reading quiz (found on http://www.quia.com) closes at 9:00 am on the date that the reading is listed.
  • Weekly programming assignments (PSAs) are due Tuesdays at 11:59pm.  Late assignments (even 1 minute late), for any reason, will be charged a slip day if you have any left. No late work will be accepted.
